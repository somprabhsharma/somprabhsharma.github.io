About Me

A introduction of my self

Hi, I am Som Prabh Sharma, a Full Stack Developer from Jaipur. Currently working as Senior Software Engineer at Crowdfire, Navi Mumbai. I passionately move pixels and lines of code to build highly reliable, scalable & maintainable systems along with clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites & apps. Some people call what I do is Software Development, some call it Web Development, some think I am a Back-End Developer, others see me as a Programmer. I see what I do is a combination of all these things. And ultimately I don’t think it really matters which label you like to use. What does matters is the output of my work, which is always focused at the point where user, business and technical requirements meet and tend to become a perfect product.

I have a wide range of skills that include back-end technologies (Go, Node, Java, C, Express, Hapi, Gin, Struts), databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL, Aerospike, Elasticsearch, MongoDB, Redis, DynamoDB), front-end technologies (ReactJS, AngularJs, Bootstrap, Materialize, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript) and other concepts & technologies (Kubernetes, Docker, AWS, Kibana, SQS, Microservice Architecture, GoCD, Redash, Git, Mixpanel, New relic).

My current weapons of choice are Go, Node, React, PostgresSQL, MongoDB, Redis, Kubernetes, Docker. Being both a backend and frontend developer, I help others and myself to bring their ideas into reality.

When I am not coding or pushing pixels, you'll find me watching movies, listening to music or reading novels.
